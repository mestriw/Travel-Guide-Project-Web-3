About our project:

We, second-year students of AITU, created a website on the topic of travel! Our project is a guidebook containing tips, routes, and reviews of the places we dream of visiting. Here you will find useful information about attractions, ideal travel routes, as well as personal impressions and reviews. Our site was created to help you get inspired for new trips and discover more wonderful places in the world.
